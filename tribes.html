Who are the other characters in this story world?

Full Cast Tribes

Several characters in the Go Girl Chronicles universe belong to one of three tribes.

The Firestarters

The Firestarters are a tribe of warmongers intent on dominating the world by fostering division. They revel in using fire to destroy rather than create. Incinerating documents, for example, is a key way they have turned allies on each other throughout history.

The Cartokinetics

The Firestarters biggest adversaries are the Cartokinetics. The Cartokinetics began as a rebel faction on the island of Hispaniola who fought but failed to unite the Dominican Republic and Haiti under colonialism. Their descendants remain on this mission to resolve civil unrest across the globe and defeating those who benefit from it.

The Chameleons

Then there are the Chameleons, shape-shifters charged with protecting the Cartokinetics.

The Muggles

Not everyone can have super powers. The Muggles are regular humans that find themselves in the middle of the Go Girl Chronicles.
