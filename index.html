What would happen if every thing you drew came true?

When Lela gets a handle on her cartokinesis, will she reunite her parents and save the world? Or is she just going to stick it to her rival Josie?

It turns out those two goals are one and the same. Blending live action with animation, The Go Girl Chronicles is a webseries for tweens and teens with a realistic twist on the superhero genre because being an undocumented teen in a Dream Act world puts a whole new spin on adolescent angst.
